Entrepreneurship Awareness Drive

The Entrepreneurship Awareness Drive, one of the massive flagship events undertaken by the Entrepreneurship Cell, IIT Kharagpur is a one of its kind initiative held in 20+ cities consistently over the years across the nation in order to stimulate the spirit of entrepreneurship and encourage students to embrace the idea of starting their own venture. Held in the first fortnight of October in a reputed college of the respective city in the regular offline scenario, it consists of a series of guest lectures by distinguished entrepreneurs, termed as Entrepreneurs Awareness Camps followed by a panel discussion and a workshop. P.S. Keeping in view the pandemic, EAD 2021 will be hosted virtually over the month of October in collaboration with reputed colleges serving as Educational Partners of the respective city. Various events that you can be part of during the event are:




  • Guest Lectures

  • Workshops

  • Panel Discussion

Local Startup Meet

Entrepreneurship Cell, IIT Kharagpur has been successful in influencing students who are enthusiastic towards the startup ecosystem since 2006. With our aim to influence people who have already ventured into creating their own enterprise we proudly launch the 3rd chapter of flagship initiative Local Startups’ Meet 2021. Local Startups’ Meet is an Invite-only, well-curated event of about 40-60 people in the regular offline scenario, chiefly embodying eminent Investors, venture capitalists, Influencers along with the early-stage startups and some incubated startups that are currently working on their Alpha or Beta stage of product development. The event is designed to provide startups in Tier 1 cities with Networking and pitching opportunities. P.S. Keeping in view the pandemic, LSM 2021 will be hosted virtually over the month of October. Given the absence of Geographical barriers, start-up enthusiasts across the nation are invited to participate. The event comprises the following sessions:




  • Networking Session

  • Sector-wise Pitching Session for Start-ups

  • Panel Discussions
